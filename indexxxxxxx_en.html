Why HTML beats Markdown beats？

Markdown

• No spatial relationships
• Limited table formatting
• No interactivity
• Limited layout

HTML

• SVG charts、flowcharts
• Form Validation、animation、toggles
• Diff highlighting、line numbers、annotations
• Precise typography
